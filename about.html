About Me

I'm a software engineer with a passion for web development. I've been working in the field for several years, and I'm constantly looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow and improve.

I have a strong background in web development, with extensive experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, as well as mobile app development using Swift and SwiftUI. My MacBook Pro is my trusty companion, and I'm constantly learning new tools and technologies to stay at the forefront of the field.

I have numerous Codecademy certificates, which I earned through hard work and dedication. I'm always looking to improve my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in the industry.

I also have experience working with popular platforms like Shopify and WordPress, and I enjoy finding creative solutions to complex problems.

When I'm not coding, you can usually find me at my local CrossFit gym. I'm a dedicated athlete, and I love the challenge and sense of accomplishment that comes from pushing myself to new heights. I also have a soft spot for my French bulldog, Daxter, who always manages to brighten my day with his playful antics.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, reading books, and practicing my guitar playing.
